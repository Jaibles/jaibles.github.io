About me

I’m an experienced product designer, responsible for leading the design process on product teams, defining product direction, prioritising cross-company efforts, and shaping new practices within organisations.

As a design generalist, I use a breadth of skills to contribute to the entire product lifecycle — research, ideation, journey mapping, visual & interaction design, prototyping, measuring success, brand design, and product marketing.

New opportunities

I live in Dublin, Ireland and have been working as a freelance designer for 2 years after several years on the leadership team in house on a SaaS app. I'm always open to having a discussion about new projects and opportunities so if you're looking to start something, or have a project coming up, don't hesitate to email me at justciaranhanrahan@gmail.com!
