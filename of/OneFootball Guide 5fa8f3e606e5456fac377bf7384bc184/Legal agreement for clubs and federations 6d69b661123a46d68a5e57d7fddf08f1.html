ONEFOOTBALL NETWORK TERMS OF USE

1. Scope

1.1 OneFootball develops, operates and markets internationally the most comprehensive media platform for football fans. OneFootball provides the mobile apps "OneFootball" on iOS, Android and Harmony OS (" Apps") as well as a web version "www.onefootball.com", where a football fan reads the latest news of their favourite clubs and leagues, gets results of all the games, watches videos with relevant football content, can follow their favorite entities and receive push notifications of breaking news (together, the " Platforms"). OneFootball strives to combine selected authentic and emotional content for fans on the Platforms.

The OneFootball Club Network is OneFootball's content platform for professional football clubs. Through the OneFootball Club Network, OneFootball provides clubs with direct digital access to their existing fan base and potential new audiences via the segmented, personalised delivery of official club content.

1.2 Any professional football club that registers to the OneFootball Club Network (" Club") focuses on reaching its fans via many different digital media outlets to engage them off the pitch as well as on it. To this end, Club operates an official website (" Site") as well as different social media channels (e.g. Facebook, Instagram, TikTok – together, the " Social Media Channels") with official club content. The content may vary and consist of, including, but not limited to, articles, texts, information, photographs, illustrations, videos and/or captions (" Club** Content**").

1.3 The Club seeks to integrate the Club Content from the Site and the Social Media Channels into the Platforms in order for any Club's fan who is a OneFootball user to be able to access the Club Content on the Platforms as well.

2. Term

Club shall be bound by these Terms of Use (" TOU") upon registration for the Club Network. The Club's registration can be terminated any time by either party in text form (email being sufficient) with a notice period of one month; Club shall send any notice to the following email address: network@onefootball.com. The right to terminate the contract for good cause with immediate effect shall be unaffected thereby.

3. Use of Club Content

3.1 Transmission of Club Content

Club makes the Club Content available to OneFootball via an API (e.g. WordPress REST API, OneFootball Network API, Club's own API) or by letting OneFootball scrape Club's Site. Club agrees to make the Club Content available at all times except for maintenance on the Site. Club agrees to remedy any problems appearing with the delivery of the Club Content without undue delay.

OneFootball aims to further include Club Content from the Club's Social Media Channels (" Club's Social Media Content") on the Platforms, once the technical environment is set up. The Parties agree that any such inclusion of Club's Social Media Content is subject to further consent of the Club.

3.2 Format and Club Content

a) The Club Content will be distributed accordingly on the Platforms. Club is not entitled to any specific placement of the Club Content within the Platforms, especially not regarding a placement within the first articles of curated collections and/or clubs/leagues/players/competitions pages, except for an appearance in the Club's stream that is generated for OneFootball's users that have chosen to follow the Club (" Club Stream").

b) For any of the Club Content, in whole or in part, that appears on the Platforms, OneFootball will

  • include attribution to Club in a manner to be determined by OneFootball, including but not limited to the logo (to be uploaded by Club, " Logo") and the Club's name and
  • tag it automatically with the Club.

c) Any Club Content shall have a link, provided by OneFootball, to the legal notice of Club's Site.

3.3 Reservation of Rights

a) OneFootball reserves the right but is not obligated to remove Club Content that is likely to violate the provisions of these TOU.

b) OneFootball reserves the right to change and improve the Platforms.

c) OneFootball reserves the right to extend the distribution of Club Content within the Platforms beyond the Club Stream, including but not limited to sending a push notification or including it in curated or automated content collections.

d) OneFootball reserves the right to remove content, that contains advertising, sponsoring, product placement and similar promotional content (“branded content”) from the platform.

3.4 Marketing

OneFootball will refrain from adding any advertisement to the Club Content, unless agreed otherwise.

3.5 Reporting

OneFootball will provide a regular report on Club Content read/seen within the Platforms.

4. Fees

The Parties agree that no fees shall apply for neither party.

5. Rights

5.1 Neither party will acquire any intellectual property rights under these TOU, except for the licenses as specified hereinafter.

5.2 Club grants OneFootball a non-exclusive, revocable, non-transferable, non-assignable, worldwide license to use

  • its Logo and name limited to the acts that present the Club and the Club Content on the Platforms in the best possible manner to OneFootball's users as well as for the use in, including, but not limited to, connection with the promotion of the Club Content and/or the Platforms;
  • the Club Content on the Platforms for the duration of the Term, so that the Club Content can be placed and marketed on the Platforms in accordance with these TOU as well as for the use in, including, but not limited to, connection with the promotion of the Club Content and/or the Platforms.

6. Prohibited Club Content

Club ensures and guarantees that none of the Club Content is pornographic, violent, hateful, illegal or rights-infringing. In the case that third parties alert OneFootball of violations of rights (in particular copyright, personal rights) by the Club three times or more, OneFootball has the right to terminate this Agreement with immediate effect. Furthermore, it is prohibited to make available unwanted, misleading or repetitive content (" Spam").
