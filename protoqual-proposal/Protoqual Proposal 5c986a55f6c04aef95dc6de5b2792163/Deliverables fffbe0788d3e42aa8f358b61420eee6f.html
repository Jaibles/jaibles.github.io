Approach

We have outlined a 6 phase approach and created a timeline specific to the project that will ensure we meet the needs of the project and deliver on time.

Phase 1 - Research

The research phase helps us to define the goals and objectives for the new St. Kilian’s website. We will start by doing a requirement analysis which will help us identify the needs of current students, current parents, prospective parents and staff.

Requirement analysis, user analysis, competitor analysis, questionnaire, creative brief

Phase 2 - Planning

All the good solid work from phase one will ensure that we hit all the correct marks when planning the website from a functional and visual perspective. We will prepare a sitemap and a general layout for the website that is most appropriate for the needs of your target audience.

We will also create a content timeline which will outline what needs to be prepared and when. We will help you to work on your current content and ensure that you have all the guidelines you need for optimising it for your new website.

We’ve proposed an initial version of the sitemap here based on our own ideas and the sitemap suggested in the tender document.

Sitemap & information architecture, wireframes, user flows, content planning, detailed functionality

Phase 3 - Design

From the previous phases we have identified exactly what we need to do and now we start to visualise our ideas for your website. The visual identity is built upon and adheres to the brand identity manual and layout for the website starts to take shape and by the end of this phase we will have a fully presentable pdf version of your new website and visual identity.

We create an online brand styleguide and make it available on your website accessible to all staff with everything they need easily downloadable. Building this visual identity is extremely important for visualising the unique ideals, cultures and life in the school and transforming the website into something that truly represents St. Kilian’s. It also ensures that the work we do stands the test of time. The school can keep its visual identity consistent for years to come. e.g. headfort.com/brand

ui styleguide, online brand styleguide, detailed wireframes & mockups (full site designs)
